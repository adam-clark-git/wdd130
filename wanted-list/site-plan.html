The FBI's most wanted list has existed since the 1950s, so that news media could inform the public of dangerous criminals the FBI wants to catch. Ever since, it's been a massively helpful tool, with civilians helping capture 163 fugitives. Here are the current list of the ten most wanted fugitives.
